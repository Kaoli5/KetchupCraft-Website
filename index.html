About KetchupCraft.

IP - play.ketchupcraft.com

Ketchupcraft was founded on the principle of community interaction and professionalism. We operate differently than a generic startup server, and we offer an array of gamemodes that provide immense entertainment.

We like to have fun on the staff team, but when it comes down to business we get serious and accomplish the task with amazing work ethic and communication skills. We always strive to learn more and are taught the importance of patience and kindness throughout the community.

Our playerbase is always growing and we really enjoy interacting with players. We put the players first! KC Believes in listening to the players and what they want, as this fosters a positive relationship between the staff and playerbase.

With emphasis on passive/single-player gamemodes, we can provide a very peaceful minecraft experience to anyone who comes on. And with gamemodes being thought up often, we have the experience to implement any gamemode the players want!
